FAQ

Who are we?

We are a team of hackers and organizers with an ambitious vision to bring together over 2000+ hackers from around the world for 40 hours. We're expecting big things.

Why are we doing this?

We believe in high schoolers - high school students deserve to have a way to express their ideas in a professional environment, as college students and adults often have.

Where and when is InFuture taking place?

In the Greater New York City area this August! InFuture, InFuture, InFuture, InFuture, InFuture, InFuture!

Who can apply?

Any student, up to and including the high school graduating class, is welcome to apply! Anyone in college is welcome to apply as a mentor. If you're unsure if you are eligible, shoot us an email at hello@infuture.io.

How is this different from other hackathons?

This event is more than just a hackathon. The hackathon is just a portion of the InFuture experience. Workshops, talks, games, networking opportunities are a major focus and share a large portion of the itinerary.

Will there be travel reimbursement?

Depending on your location and your needs, we can reimburse you up to 100% of your travel costs.
